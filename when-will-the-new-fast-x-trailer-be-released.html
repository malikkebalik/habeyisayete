When Will the New Fast X Trailer Be Released?

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Fast X’s debut trailer, which star Vin Diesel has been hinting will be released soon, will eventually be released on February 10. This is the latest installment in the Fast and Furious franchise.

Fast X is the tenth film in the Fast and Furious series and the second-to-last film in the franchise before Dominic Toretto and company’s journey is declared complete, therefore the timing is hardly coincidental. Diesel’s Instagram posts suggested a Super Bowl release date, but it appears Universal passed on the opportunity, possibly to avoid competing with the trailer for DC’s The Flash, which has already been scheduled to air on that Sunday.

According to an exclusive report by Collider, the release date of January 10 has been confirmed, and the length of the Fast X teaser might be over 3 minutes, giving fans a good idea of the kind of mischief Toretto’s team will be getting into. Now, while the major trailer won’t air until after the Super Bowl, Fast X will still have a TV spot air on the night of the event that may serve as a teaser for the entire trailer.

It’s hardly surprising that Fast X would continue some sort of promotion on the Super Bowl date, given that F9’s initial trailer debuted during the game and was instrumental in reviving the movie industry after the epidemic. It’s hardly unexpected that a lengthy trailer is required to introduce new cast members like Brie Larson and Jason Momoa in this installment of the Fast and Furious franchise.
