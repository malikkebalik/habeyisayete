Roxanne DeJesus Shares Heartbreaking News

Publish date: 2024-08-21

If you follow Teen Mom news at all, you probably know about the chaotic spinoff plans that have been unfolding recently.

MTV had an idea to cast  Teen Mom OG, Teen Mom 2, and Teen Mom: Young & Pregnant girls in a spinoff series focused around therapy and healing. All the moms were to stay in a hotel together and participate in a mental health retreat of sorts, but apparently, it’s already a disaster!

Some of the cast members started to gather on the evening of Tuesday, September 14, but they’re already at odds with one another.

Rumor has it Briana DeJesus, Brittany DeJesus, and Jade Cline have already been kicked out of their living quarters after getting into a fight with Ashley Jones.

Now, Roxanne has shared she’s done!
