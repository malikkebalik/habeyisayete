The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Dianas Unexpected Connection to Bill Revealed But Does S

Publish date: 2024-07-05

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal that more information has surfaced about Kate Schettler’s role as Diana on the CBS soap. When Schettler first appeared in the credits along with her character’s name, fans started buzzing about the possibility of her portraying Diana Carter, Sheila Carter’s (Kimberlin Brown) daughter.

Now Soap Opera Network is reporting that Schettler will be playing a realtor who shares scenes with Bill Spencer (Don Diamont) regarding the Malibu beach house.

It sounds like Diana will help Bill take care of the paperwork for the lease and make RJ Forrester’s (Joshua Hoffman) rental official.

Of course, that doesn’t necessarily mean that Schettler’s Diana isn’t Diana Carter.

It would actually be a perfect sneaky way to introduce Sheila’s daughter to the canvas if Bill is unknowingly using her as his realtor!

Diana’s last name hasn’t been revealed yet, but we can assume she isn’t using “Carter” since it apparently hasn’t raised any red flags for Bill.

Regardless, Diana might be a realtor with a big secret if she’s hiding the fact that none other than Sheila Carter is her mother.

It’s worth mentioning that Kimberlin Brown’s mind was blown over a script recently – one that seemingly featured the name “Diana” on the front.

Brown shared a video on Instagram to hype up her upcoming story, which included the audio for Taylor Swift’s “…Ready For It?”

Since this B&B script appears to be for an episode that’s coming up down the road, that could indicate that Diana will be sticking around – or at least that she’ll appear again.

It’d be interesting if fans meet Diana now thinking she’s just some random realtor and then get the Sheila mother-daughter revelation later.

Whatever the case, there’s an awful lot of Diana talk going on – and it can’t be ignored that Sheila’s daughter shares that name.

Could that mean there’ll be more than meets the eye when it comes to Bill’s realtor?
