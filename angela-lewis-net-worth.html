Angela Lewis is an actress. She had her career beginnings in the mid-2000s when she acted in short films. In 2009, she made her film debut with a brief role in Today’s Special. She also had an uncredited role in the movie Fair Game. Later in 2013, she had her first guest role in an episode of The Good Wife. That same year, she also guest-starred in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, as well as starred in the TV film The Ordained. After guest-starred in The Last Ship and Code Black, Lewis starred as Louise Saint in the television series Snowfall.
