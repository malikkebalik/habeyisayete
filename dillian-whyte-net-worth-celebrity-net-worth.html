What is Dillian Whyte's Net Worth?

Dillian Whyte is a British professional boxer who has a net worth of $7 million. Dillian Whyte was born in Port Antonio, Portland, Jamaica in April 1988. He is a heavyweight who has also competed as a super-heavyweight in kickboxing. Whyte made his professional boxing debut with a win over Tayar Mehmed in May 2011. In September 2015 he won the vacant WBC Silver International heavyweight title with a win over Brian Minto. Whyte suffered his first loss in a match for the WBC International, Commonwealth, and vacant British heavyweight titles against Anthony Joshua in December 2015. In July 2016 he won the vacant WBC International heavyweight title with a win over David Allen and in October 2016 he defeated Ian Lewison to win the vacant British heavyweight title. Dillian Whyte won the vacant WBC Silver heavyweight title in October 2017 when he defeated Robert Helenius. He won the vacant WBO International heavyweight title by beating Joseph Parker in July 2018.
