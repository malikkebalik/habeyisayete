Control Z Actress Macarena Garcia Romero Wiki | Bio

Macarena Garcia Romero (born on October 26, 2000) is a 21 years old actress and the daughter of famous Mexica artist Amairani Romero. In 2022, Garcia became the center of the spotlight after the Netflix show “Control Z Season 3” got released on July 6. In 2022, the third installment of the series came out, whereas the first came in 2020. Macarena has been part of this production since the release of its first season.

The actress was a teenager when she first entered the Mexican TV industry. Romero’s first show was a Lili Garza-directed romantic drama, “Valiant Love,” where her role was Ana Albarrán. You might have seen in many other programs like “Como dice el dicho,” “Sin Miedo a la Verdad,” “Los elegidos” and “Decisions: Some Win and Others Lose.”

Early Life

On October 26, 2000, Macarena Garcia Romero (age 21 years) came to this world after her birth in Mexico, Distrito Federal, Mexico. Her mom, Amairani Romero, is a famous actress and has worked for over a decade in the Mexican film industry. Garcia also has a younger sister named Fernanda and a brother named Juan Luis Arias.

Many people are unaware that Romero’s grandmother Anabelle Gutiérrez is seen as a legend in the Mexican acting world. Carrying on her family’s tradition, Garcia also decided to become an actor and was engaged in learning the art from a young age. When she turned 11, she got to play a recurring role in a show that started her career.

On the one hand, she was shooting for serials every day; on the other hand, she had schooling. It wasn’t easy to carry both of them together, but the young girl did it and completed her graduation with flying colors.

Career

Although Romero started working as an actor in 2012, after the first show, she appeared on television only in 2017. It is unknown why there was a gap of 5 years in between, but when Garcia returned, she emerged as a star in the TV world. “Como dice el dicho” was the second show Romero starred in. She appeared in only two episodes, but in the same year, her performance in “Papis muy padres” won the hearts of millions.

In this popular production Rodrigo Curiel filmed Garcia alongside Dulce María, Víctor González, Héctor Suárez Gomiz, Mariel Molino, Roberto Mateos and others. As soon as Romero rose to fame, she got offers for many brand commercials. Big brands like Maybelline, Secret, and Benetton began promoting their products through Makrana’s social media accounts.

Over time, this Mexican beauty has worked in many other shows as well, like “100 días para Enamorarnos,” “Decisions: Some Win and Others Lose,” “Like La Leyenda,” and “Los elegidos.”
