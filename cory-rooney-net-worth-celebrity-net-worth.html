What is Cory Rooney's Net Worth?

Cory Rooney is an American songwriter, record producer, and C.E.O. who has a net worth of $12 million. Cory Rooney is the C.E.O. of Cory Rooney Group and has written, developed, and produced successful songs and careers for artists such as Mary J. Blige, Jennifer Lopez, Mariah Carey, Jessica Simpson, Thalia, Destiny's Child, and Marc Anthony. Rooney's parents were part of the band The Exciters who toured with The Beatles. His father went into production and had a hit with the Isley Brothers "It's Your Thing". As a teenager Cory joined the band The Exciters.

Rooney and Prince Markie Dee of the Fat Boys released two solo albums by Dee in the 1990s including the #1 Billboard Rap single "Typical Reasons (Swing My Way)".
