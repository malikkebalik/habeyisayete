What is Malcolm Miles' Net Worth?

Malcolm Miles is an American music executive who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Malcolm Miles is based in Los Angeles, California and began his professional career in the music industry many years ago. He began to make waves in 2000 as Senior Director of Promotions at J Records, where he worked with such clients as Maroon 5, Wyclef Jean, Angie Stone, and Alicia Keys. After four years at J Records, he became Vice President of Promotions for Sony Urban Edit, part of Sony Music Entertainment.

He spent two years working with such artists as, Mary Mary, Nas, Ginuwine, DMX, and John Legend. Since 2006, he has served as Junior Vice President of Promotions at Young Money/Cash Money Records. He has been instrumental in the promotion and success of Lil Wayne's albums, "Tha Carter III" and "Tha Carter IV", and he was also involved in the debut albums of Nicki Minaj and Drake.
