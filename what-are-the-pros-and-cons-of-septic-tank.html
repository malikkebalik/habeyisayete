The most common disadvantages of septic systems are: In some cases, septic tanks may lead to contamination in water which may be used for human use and consumption. In this way, is it better to have a septic tank or sewer? If a neighborhood is outside the area serviced by the local sewer system, the homes will generally use a septic system to handle waste water.
