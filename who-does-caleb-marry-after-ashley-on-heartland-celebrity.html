Cindy Busby is a French Canadian actress who plays Ashley Stanton in the hit TV series Heartland. She was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, and is also a Hallmark Channel favorite actress. The early seasons saw Ashley as one of the most hated characters of the show, which changes drastically once she begins her relationship with Caleb Odell.

Her favorite movies of all time are Groundhog Day (1993) and the Back to the Future franchise. Her favorite book of all time is “The Alchemist” by Paulo Coelho. She also loved the Heartland books by Lauren Brooke. If Cindy Busby wasn’t an actress, she would be a professional organizer.
