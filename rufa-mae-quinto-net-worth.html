What is Rufa Mae Quinto's Net Worth?

Rufa Mae Quinto is a Filipina-American actress, host, musician, and comedian who has a net worth of $5 million. Born in 1978, Rufa Mae Quinto began her on-screen career as the host of "That's Entertainment" during the mid-'90s on the GMA Network. In 1997, she landed her first big break, playing Booba and Boobita Rose on "Gloria, Gloria Labandera", which became a phenomenon in the Philippines. Quinto frequently hosts and appears on shows on the GMA Network, including "The Ryzza Mae Show", "Sarap Diva", "Vampire Ang Daddy Ko", "Eat Bulaga!", "Motorcycle Diaries", "14 Going Steady: Bubble Gang's 14th Anniversary Special", "Whammy! Push Your Luck", "Showbiz Central", "Hokus Pokus", "SOP Rules", "Kool Ka Lang", and "Anna Karenina".

She has won a handful of PMPC Star Awards, including Best Comedy Actress for "Bubble Gang" and "Ispup". She also won Supporting Actress in 2000 for "Dahil May Isang Ikaw". She has appeared in top movies such as "Manay Po 2", "I.T.A.L.Y.", "Desperadas", "Desperadas 2", "Pasukob", and "Apat-Dapat Dapat-Apat". Quinto formerly dated popular Filipino singer and actor Erik Santos, who won the "Star In A Million" TV show. She made headlines in 2007 after being the victim of a high-profile burglary in Quezon City in the Philippines, where several of her possessions were stolen.
