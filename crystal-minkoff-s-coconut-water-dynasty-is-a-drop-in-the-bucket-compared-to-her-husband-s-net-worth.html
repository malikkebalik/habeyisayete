Crystal Minkoff's Coconut Water Dynasty Is a Drop in the Bucket Compared to Her Husband's Net Worth

Crystal Minkoff is a cast member on 'Real Housewives of Beverly Hills' — and her husband has an impressive net worth.

Published on June 11, 2021

2 min read

Large houses, expensive designer outfits, and private planes make up the necessity of joining the exclusive Bravo housewives club. This is why Crystal Kung Minkoff didn’t have any trouble fitting into the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills crowd. She not only has all of the listed requirements, but she also has a famous and wealthy husband to go with it.

Minkoff has a coconut water empire that seems like a drop in the bucket if placed alongside her husband’s net worth. Find out how wealthy Minkoff and her husband are and how great her dynasty is.

Minkoff’s arrival to ‘RHOBH’ made the show the first diverse one of its kind

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills season eleven premiered in May 2021, and with it came all sorts of surprises. The new season saw the addition of two cast members Kathy Hilton (Paris Hilton’s mother) and Minkoff.

Producers announced that Minkoff would be joining the show in October 2020 as a Housewife. Hilton first joined the show as a ‘Friend of the Housewives.’ Minkoff’s addition to the elite group came after Denise Richards and Teddi Mellencamp Arroyave announced that they wouldn’t be returning for the eleventh season.

This season follows Kyle Richards, Lisa Rinna, Erika Girardi, Dorit Kemsley and Garcelle Beauvais. Garcelle was the first Black cast member in RHOBH, and Minkoff became the first Asian American Housewife on the show. The two new cast members have made the show the first diverse show in the Real Housewives franchise yet.

What is Crystal Minkoff’s net worth?

The fact that Minkoff is part of the Real Housewives means that she must be somewhat loaded. Aside from being a Housewife, Minkoff has a thriving business. She is the co-founder of a beverage company called Real Coco, a company she started with her brother Jeffrey Kung. The company sells premium organic beverages, and all its products are plant-based.

Real Coco hasn’t been around for a long time, but it seems to be doing just fine. The brand sells various products such as organic coconut milk, coconut chips, organic coffee creamer variations, and organic coconut water variations. The products fall within the range of $29 to $42, as reported by Heavy. Minkoff isn’t the only Housewife to have launched a water brand. Kelly Dodd became part owner of Positive Beverage late last year.

Minkoff’s husband Rob has been involved with various successful productions. He directed the 1994 Lion King movie, which grossed more than $900 million in the box office. He has also worked on Stuart Little 1 and Stuart Little 2 and The Mr. Peabody and Sherman Show.

Although the exact figure of the new RHOBH cast member isn’t readily available, sources estimate her and her husband’s combined net worth to be $16 million.

Minkoff’s mansion has significant meaning to her

The Housewives are known for having huge houses and living affluent lifestyles, and Minkoff seems to be fitting right at home. The businesswoman revealed in an interview that she and her producer/director husband built their mansion themselves, calling it “a total labor of love.”

The couple’s Beverly Hills mansion is about 9000 square feet and has been shown numerous times in the show. Minkoff stated that she wanted a home that was 8888 square feet since the number 8 has significant importance to Chinese culture. Although they didn’t find exactly what they were looking for, they cut in close with their home.
