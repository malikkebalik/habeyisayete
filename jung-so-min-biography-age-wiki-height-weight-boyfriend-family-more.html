2010 saw Jung So-Min make her television series debut in a small part in “Bad Guy.” She gained a lot of attention from her debut and went on to play a major part in the romance serial “Playful Kiss,” which turned out to be a game-changer for her career.

She was picked for the difficult part of “Can We Get Married?” in the 2012 comedy. She appeared in the one-episode drama “Came to Me and Became a Star” in 2013. She was cast in several genre-spanning television shows and motion pictures in the next years.

She appeared in the films “Daddy You, Daughter Me” and “My Father is Strange” with Yoon Je-moon. Speaking about her accomplishments, she was named the 18th Korean Culture and Entertainment Awards’ “Best New Actress for TV” winner for her role in “Bad Guy.”

She received a nomination for the 2010 MBC Drama Awards in the “Best New Actress” category for her role in “Playful Kiss.” She received nominations in 2017 for her performance in “My Father is Strange” in many categories.
