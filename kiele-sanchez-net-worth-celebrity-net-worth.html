What is Kiele Sanchez's Net Worth?

Kiele Sanchez is an American actress who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Born in Chicago, Illinois, in 1976, Kiele Sanchez started acting in high school even though she suffered from stage fright. She attempted to break into show business by auditioning to become an MTV VJ, but she wasn't selected after advancing to the final five. Soon after, she made her on-screen debut in "Live Girls" and "Migrating Forms" in 2000. Sanchez appeared in "Stuck on You" and the third season of "Lost" as Nikki Fernandez. Sanchez went on to score parts in "Married to the Kellys", "Samantha Who?", "Redemption Road", and "30 Days of Night: Dark Days" before landing a starring role as Callie Cargill in "The Glades". Sanchez starred in close to 50 episodes between 2010 and 2013. Her other TV and film credits include "Four Kings", "Modern Men", "Girls on the Bus", "Football Wives", "Mr. Magorium's Wonder Emporium", "The End of Steve", "A Perfect Getaway", "The Matadors", "Redemption Road", "Burn Notice: The Fall of Sam Axe", "The Purge: Anarchy", "Stealing Time", "Migrating Forms", and "Class Warfare". Sanchez has been married twice, first to writer and director Zach Helm, and later to fellow "The Matadors" actor Zach Gilford. They have been married since 2012.
