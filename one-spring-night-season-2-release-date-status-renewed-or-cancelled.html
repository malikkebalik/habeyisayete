Through this process, they are forced to look at their relationship from an entirely new perspective. In contrast to Lee Jung-in, Yu Ji-ho is pleasant, warm, and well-mannered, never hesitating when it comes to expressing her feelings for a man.

Every day begins and ends with the same pattern for the pair. However, both of them must discover what it is that they genuinely desire.
