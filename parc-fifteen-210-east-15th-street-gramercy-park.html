Description of Parc Fifteen at 210 East 15th Street

210 East 15th Street was built in 1964 and converted to co-op apartments in 1984. Parc Fifteen has a total of 187 apartments and is located in Gramercy Park, very close to the N, Q, R, 4, 5, 6 and L subway lines.

During the past two years, 15 apartments have sold: the most expensive was a one bedroom that sold for $1,075,000, and the least expensive was a one bedroom that sold for $515,000.

Amenities at this post war building include doorman, fitness center, rooftop terrace and full service garage.

Similar nearby buildings include 145 East 15th Street, 142 East 16th Street and 201 East 17th Street.
