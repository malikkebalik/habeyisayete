What is Jascha Washington's Net Worth?

Jascha Washington is an American actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Jascha Washington was born in Kings, California in June 1989. He made his television debut in 1997 in episodes of the TV series ER and Brooklyn South.

In 2004 Washington starred as Nate in the television series Da Boom Crew. He has starred in the movies Enemy of the State, The Wood, Dancing in September, 3 Strikes, Big Momma's House, The Visit, Snow Dogs, Antwone Fisher, Last Holiday, Big Momma's House 2, and The Final.
