Yara Martinez Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Yara Martinez net worth:

$1 million

Yara Martinez is an American actress whose career started in 2006. She made her acting debut on the TV film Faceless. Her first recurring role was on Vanished. She then made her feature film debut in The Hitcher. Around the same time, she played recurring character Annie on The Unit. She continued acting in a couple of shows, as well as TV pilots. In the 2010s, she had recurring parts on Southland and Breakout Kings. Her first regular role was on Hollywood Heights. She currently stars on the TV series Alpha House, as well as plays Dr. Luisa Alver on Jane the Virgin.
