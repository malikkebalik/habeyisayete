Julie Warner is an American actress whose career began in the 1980s. Her first starring on television was as Amy Sherman in NBC’s Pride & Joy. She later starred as Danni Lipton in CBS’ Family Law. In the ’90s, she had roles in such films as Flatliners, Doc Hollywood, and Indian Summer, among others. She went on to play Megan O’Hara, a recurring character in FX’s Nip/Tuck, as well as played Andrea Schillo in several episodes of Crash. Warner also made notable guest roles in various TV shows, including Leap Year, Dexter, and Code Black. Her latest work was in the 2020 film Unbelievable!!!!!
